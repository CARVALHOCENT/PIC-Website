The Challenge

Devastating Wildfires

Every year, wildfires destroy millions of hectares of forest, threatening biodiversity, human lives, and our climate. Traditional detection methods are slow and often too late.

Late detection leads to uncontrollable fires

Limited coverage in remote forest areas

High operational costs for monitoring

Our Solution

Smart Detection Network

ElectroCap deploys a network of intelligent IoT sensors across forests, providing real-time monitoring and early fire detection through advanced LoRaWAN technology.

Multi-Sensor Detection

Temperature, humidity, and smoke sensors working in harmony.

LoRaWAN Connectivity

Long-range, low-power communication up to 15km.

Sustainable Design

Solar-powered units with minimal environmental impact.

Technical Excellence

MVP Features

Our minimum viable product demonstrates the core capabilities of the ElectroCap system

Real-time Monitoring

Continuous surveillance with instant alerts when anomalies are detected. Data visualization through our cloud dashboard.

Long-range Communication

LoRa technology enables sensor networks spanning up to 15km, perfect for vast forest areas with minimal infrastructure.

Low Power Consumption

Solar-powered sensors with optimized energy usage ensure years of operation without maintenance in remote locations.
